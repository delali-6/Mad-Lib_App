👥 How Team Mode Works

  1. Pick a story and tap Team Mode on the play page.
  2. Player 1 sees only their blank — nobody else peeks!
  3. They fill it in, tap Next Player →, then pass the device.
  4. Repeat until all blanks are filled — then hit Generate!
  5. Read the story out loud and enjoy the chaos together. 🎉
